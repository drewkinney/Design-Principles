# Visceral / Arc — Experience / Emotion

Emotion is a design tool. Curiosity, anticipation, and excitement prime the Behavioral layer.
The emotional arc is designed, not left to emerge from layout decisions.
What the user feels at first contact determines what they're willing to do next.

Score: PASSES | FAILS | MIXED | UNSCORED

---

## Evaluate

- Does first contact produce the intended emotional state from PRE-AUDIT.md?
- Is there a designed emotional arc from landing — or a single emotional moment?
- Does the interface sustain emotional engagement past the hero section?
- Do service or utility sections maintain emotional consistency with product sections?
- Does the page's progression amplify or flatten the opening emotion?

Failure modes: strong hero that flatlines on scroll, service and support pages with no
emotional arc, utility-style presentation for products requiring emotional buy-in,
emotional tone that contradicts the brand position from PRE-AUDIT.md.

PASSES: emotional arc sustained from first contact through the page; arc is designed.
MIXED: strong at entry; flat or inconsistent on secondary sections.
FAILS: emotional engagement absent or contradictory beyond the hero; feels assembled.
